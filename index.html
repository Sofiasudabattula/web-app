Chicken Tandoori

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

  • 500g chicken
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  1. Marinate the chicken with all ingredients and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  3. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray.
  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes, basting occasionally.
  5. Broil for an additional 5-10 minutes for crispy skin.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and serve hot.
